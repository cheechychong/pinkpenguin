Kestrel Park Wedding — Cathy & Logan

Santa Ynez greeted us with a golden mist that wrapped every hill in painterly light. Cathy and Logan spent the morning reading letters with their families, surrounded by loose blooms, citrus, and classic stationery.

The vows took place under a centuries-old oak where guests were welcomed with lavender lemonade. As the sun dipped, a live jazz trio led everyone to the dinner terrace for a five-course feast served under countless taper candles.

After their first dance, the couple slipped away for ten minutes of portraits on the hillside—our favorite frames from the day. Enjoy a selection below.
